Kitchen Cleaning

This high-traffic area of your home requires special attention, which a professional kitchen cleaning service can provide. SPL Cleaning housekeepers thoroughly clean your kitchen to remove all dirt, debris and grime that may have been left behind.

Carpet Cleaning

We deep clean your carpet to remove dirt, dust, allergens, and more returning it to a like new state. Whether you are moving out from a rental property or just need a restorative cleaning, our carpet cleaning services will bring new life to your dirty carpets.

House Cleaning

One of the marks of SPL Cleaning's professional cleaning service is that we clean your home with a game plan in mind. A strategy that takes on cleaning in a systematic, organized way (while having the flexibility to incorporate your custom wishes).

Office Cleaning

Keep your employees healthy and happy with a regular office cleaning. A clean and organized office improves productivity and reduces sick days. A messy office doesn’t just look unprofessional, it can also distract colleagues and decrease motivation.

Apartment Cleaning
